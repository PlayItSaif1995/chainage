Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026 · Terms of Service

This Privacy Policy explains how Chainage collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our service. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to being transparent about our data practices.

1. Information We Collect

Account information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and (where applicable) company name.

Payment information: Payment processing is handled by Stripe. We do not store your card details — Stripe holds this securely.

Usage data: We collect anonymised usage analytics to understand how the product is used and improve it. This does not include the content of your programmes.

Programme files: Your .tcplan files are saved locally on your device. If you use cloud save features, files are encrypted in transit and at rest.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the Service
  • To process payments and manage your subscription
  • To send you important account and service notifications
  • To respond to your support requests
  • To improve and develop new features (using anonymised analytics only)
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage and Security

Your account data is stored on Supabase infrastructure hosted in the EU. We use industry-standard encryption (TLS in transit, AES-256 at rest) to protect your data.

Programme files you save locally never leave your device unless you explicitly share them.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Supabase — authentication and database hosting
  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Legal authorities — only where required by law

5. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication (keeping you logged in). We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser but this will prevent you from logging in.

6. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

As a UK/EU user, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Data portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@chainage.net.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we remove your personal data within 30 days, except where we are legally required to retain it.

8. Children's Privacy

Chainage is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of significant changes by email or in-app notification. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@chainage.net.
