Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026  ·  Terms of Service

This Privacy Policy explains how Chainage collects, uses, and protects your information. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to transparency.

1. Information We Collect

Account information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and optionally your company name.

Payment information: Payment processing is handled by Stripe. We do not store your card details.

Usage data: We collect anonymised analytics to understand how the product is used. This does not include the content of your programmes.

Programme files: Your .tcplan files are saved locally. If you use cloud save features, files are encrypted in transit and at rest.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the Service
  • To process payments and manage your subscription
  • To send important account and service notifications
  • To respond to support requests
  • To improve features using anonymised analytics
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage and Security

Your account data is stored on Supabase infrastructure hosted in the EU. We use TLS encryption in transit and AES-256 at rest. Programme files you save locally never leave your device unless you explicitly share them.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with Supabase (authentication and database), Stripe (payment processing), and legal authorities where required by law.

5. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication only. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

6. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data. To exercise any right, email privacy@chainage.net.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. On deletion, we remove your personal data within 30 days.

8. Contact

Privacy questions: privacy@chainage.net.
