Welcome to Time-Chainage Planner

A professional programme tool for linear infrastructure projects.

1
Set your timeline
Go to ⚙️ Settings — set your project start/end dates and chainage range, then Apply.
2
Add activities
Go to ✏️ Activities — fill in a name, dates, chainages and click Save Activity. Or use 🔄 Sync to bulk import from Excel.
3
Organise with groups
Go to 📂 Grouping — create categories and sub-groups to colour-code and filter your programme.
4
Export & share
Use 📤 Export to download as PDF, PNG or Excel. The 📊 Dashboard gives senior leadership a one-page summary.
