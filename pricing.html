Frequently asked questions

Is there a free trial? +

Yes — all plans include a 14-day free trial with full feature access. No credit card required.

How does it compare to TILOS? +

TILOS costs thousands per seat and requires installation. Chainage is browser-based, a fraction of the cost, and designed specifically for the UK infrastructure supply chain — with Monte Carlo risk analysis built in.

Can I import from Primavera P6? +

Yes — Chainage syncs dates from P6 via Activity ID matching. Full P6 XML import is on the roadmap.

What happens to my data if I cancel? +

Your .tcplan files are saved locally — they belong to you. Cancelling ends cloud access but your files remain fully intact.
