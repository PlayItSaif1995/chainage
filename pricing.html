Frequently asked questions

Is there a free trial?+

Yes — all plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required. You get full access to every feature during the trial.

Can I use Chainage offline?+

Chainage is browser-based but your programme files (.tcplan) save to your computer. You can open and work on saved files offline — you only need a connection to log in and access cloud features.

How does it compare to TILOS?+

TILOS is powerful but expensive (£1,000s/year per seat) and requires installation. Chainage is browser-based, a fraction of the cost, and designed specifically for the UK infrastructure supply chain. It covers the core time-chainage workflow with Monte Carlo risk analysis built in.

Can I import from Primavera P6?+

Yes — Chainage supports P6 date sync via P6 ID matching. Export updated dates from P6 and merge them into your Chainage programme. Full P6 XML import is on the roadmap.

What happens to my data if I cancel?+

Your .tcplan files are saved locally on your machine — they're yours. If you cancel your subscription you lose access to cloud features, but your files remain fully intact and openable.
