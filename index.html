Features

Everything a planner actually needs

Built by a senior planner working on HS2. Every feature exists because it was missing from existing tools.

📐

Time-Chainage Diagrams

Professional canvas-based diagrams with drag, resize, multi-select and snap. All shapes — rectangles, lines, diamonds, stars, flags. Full colour control.

🔗

Dependency Links

Elbow-routed dependency arrows between activities. FS, SS, FF, SF link types with lag. Visual critical path highlighting with multi-path support.

⚠️

Risk Intelligence

Full risk register with P50/P80/P90 adjusted completion dates. Float vs risk analysis flags hidden critical paths. Mitigation tracking with overdue alerts.

📊

Programme Dashboard

One-page client-facing summary: scorecards, cost breakdown, activity load, zone progress, critical path summary, and risk heat map.

💰

Resources & Costs

Labour, equipment and material cost tracking per activity. Monthly resource histograms. S-curve generation. Cost breakdown donut charts.

📌

Baseline Comparisons

Save baselines at any point. Ghost overlay shows exactly where activities have slipped vs the original plan. Essential for commercial claims.

🏗️

Chainage Zones

Define named zones across your corridor. Activities automatically grouped by zone. Zone breakdown on the dashboard with completion percentages.

📤

Professional Exports

Export to PDF (A1/A3/A4), PNG at up to 3× resolution, or Excel. Legend bundled automatically on page 2. Title block with your project details.

🔄

P6 & Excel Sync

Bulk create activities from Excel. Sync updated dates from Primavera P6 by P6 ID. Your layout stays intact — only dates update.

Risk Intelligence

The risk register TILOS never built

Log risks, track mitigations, and see how they affect your completion date — automatically.

📅

P50 / P80 / P90 Completion Dates

Risk-adjusted completion scenarios calculated from your register. P80 is the date you commit to clients. All drawn automatically on your programme.

⚡

Float vs Risk Alerts

Automatically flags activities where a risk exceeds available float — revealing hidden critical paths before they become real problems on site.

✅

Mitigation Progress Tracking

Every risk has a mitigation action, owner, due date and progress slider. Overdue mitigations are flagged automatically on the dashboard.
